URBANCART - PAYMENT & COD POLICY
Version: Q2 2024 | Approved by: Payments & Risk, Customer Experience
Applies to: All orders placed via the UrbanCart mobile app and website in India

1. ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS
1.1 UPI, credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UrbanCart Wallet, and Cash on Delivery (COD) where available.
1.2 EMI is available on eligible credit cards for cart values above Rs 3,000.

2. CASH ON DELIVERY (COD)
2.1 COD is available only on serviceable pincodes and only for order values up to Rs 20,000.
2.2 A COD convenience fee of Rs 20 may apply and is shown at checkout. This fee is non-refundable, consistent with the Return & Refund Policy Section 5.4.
2.3 High COD-refusal-risk pincodes may require prepaid payment; this is determined automatically at checkout.
2.4 COD is not available for customised or made-to-order products.

3. COD VERIFICATION
3.1 COD orders may require mobile OTP verification before confirmation.
3.2 Unverified COD orders are held for up to 24 hours and then cancelled automatically.

4. FAILED AND PENDING PAYMENTS
4.1 If money is debited but the order is not confirmed, the amount is auto-reversed within 5 to 7 business days.
4.2 A pending payment does not reserve stock; the item may sell out before payment completes.
4.3 Customers should not re-attempt payment on a debited-but-unconfirmed order until the auto-reversal window has passed.

5. REFUNDS TO PAYMENT METHODS
5.1 Refund timelines by payment method are governed by the Return & Refund Policy, Section 4.
5.2 COD order refunds follow the Return & Refund Policy, Section 5, via bank transfer or wallet credit.

6. PRICING AND CHARGES
6.1 All prices are shown in Indian Rupees and are inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise.
6.2 The final payable amount, including any shipping or COD fees, is always displayed at checkout before payment is confirmed.
