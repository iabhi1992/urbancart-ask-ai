URBANCART - RETURN & REFUND POLICY
Version: Q2 2024 | Approved by: Customer Experience & Legal
Applies to: All orders placed via the UrbanCart mobile app and website in India

1. RETURN ELIGIBILITY BY CATEGORY
1.1 Apparel & Ethnic Wear: Returnable within 14 days of delivery, provided original tags are intact and the item is unworn and unwashed.
1.2 Electronics & Gadgets: Returnable within 7 days of delivery. Original packaging, all accessories, manuals, and the invoice must be returned with the item.
1.3 Home & Kitchen: Returnable within 10 days of delivery, unused and in original packaging.
1.4 Footwear: Returnable within 14 days of delivery, unworn, with the original shoe box undamaged.
1.5 No product category has a return window exceeding 30 days. Any listing showing a longer window is a catalog data error and must be reported to Catalog Operations.

2. CONDITIONS THAT VOID A RETURN
2.1 The item shows signs of use, wear, washing, or alteration by the customer.
2.2 Original tags, labels, or serial number stickers have been removed or tampered with.
2.3 The item was damaged after delivery due to customer mishandling.
2.4 Accessories, freebies, or promotional items received with the order are missing.
2.5 The return request is raised after the category return window in Section 1 has expired.

3. NON-RETURNABLE CATEGORIES
3.1 Innerwear, lingerie, and socks, for hygiene reasons.
3.2 Perishable and consumable goods, including food items.
3.3 Customised or made-to-order products, including personalised engraving or tailoring.
3.4 Items explicitly marked "Non-Returnable" on the product detail page at the time of purchase.

4. REFUND TIMELINES BY PAYMENT METHOD
4.1 UPI: Refund credited within 3 to 5 business days of pickup and quality check.
4.2 Credit or Debit Card: Refund credited to the original card within 5 to 7 business days of quality check.
4.3 Net Banking: Refund credited within 5 to 7 business days of quality check.
4.4 UrbanCart Wallet: Refund credited instantly upon successful quality check.
4.5 Refund timelines begin only after the returned item passes the quality check at the warehouse, not at the time of pickup.

5. REFUNDS FOR CASH ON DELIVERY (COD) ORDERS
5.1 COD orders have no original payment instrument to refund to. The customer must provide bank account details or accept UrbanCart Wallet credit.
5.2 If bank details are provided, the refund is transferred via NEFT within 7 business days of quality check.
5.3 If UrbanCart Wallet credit is chosen, the amount is credited instantly upon quality check.
5.4 COD convenience fees, where charged, are non-refundable.

6. EXCHANGE VERSUS REFUND
6.1 Exchanges are available for size or colour variants of the same product, subject to stock availability, within the category return window.
6.2 If the requested exchange variant is out of stock, the request is automatically converted to a refund.
6.3 Only one exchange is permitted per order item. A second request must be raised as a return and refund.

7. HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
7.1 Returns are initiated from the Orders screen in the UrbanCart app by selecting the item and choosing Return or Exchange.
7.2 A reverse pickup is scheduled within 2 business days for serviceable pincodes.
7.3 For non-serviceable pincodes, the customer may self-ship to the returns address, and reasonable courier charges are reimbursed as wallet credit.
