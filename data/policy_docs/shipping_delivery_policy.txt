URBANCART - SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
Version: Q2 2024 | Approved by: Logistics & Customer Experience
Applies to: All orders placed via the UrbanCart mobile app and website in India

1. DELIVERY TIMELINES BY PINCODE TIER
1.1 Tier 1 metro pincodes (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad): 2 to 4 business days.
1.2 Tier 2 city pincodes: 4 to 6 business days.
1.3 Tier 3 and rural serviceable pincodes: 6 to 9 business days.
1.4 Delivery timelines begin from the date of order confirmation, not the date the order was placed, for prepaid orders. For COD orders, timelines begin from order confirmation after COD verification.

2. SHIPPING CHARGES
2.1 Orders with a cart value of Rs 499 or above ship free across all tiers.
2.2 Orders below Rs 499 incur a flat shipping fee of Rs 40.
2.3 Bulky items in Home & Kitchen (for example large appliances) may carry an additional handling fee, shown at checkout before payment.

3. COD AVAILABILITY RULES
3.1 Cash on Delivery is available only on serviceable pincodes and only for order values up to Rs 20,000. See the Payment & COD Policy for the full COD ceiling and verification rules.
3.2 Certain categories and sellers may disable COD; availability is shown on the product detail page and at checkout.
3.3 COD is not available for customised or made-to-order products.

4. ORDER TRACKING
4.1 A tracking link is shared via app notification and SMS once the order is dispatched.
4.2 Order status values are: Confirmed, Packed, Dispatched, Out for Delivery, Delivered, and Returned.
4.3 If an order shows Delivered but has not been received, the customer must report it within 48 hours for investigation.

5. DELAYS AND FAILED DELIVERY
5.1 Deliveries may be delayed during sale events, adverse weather, or in remote pincodes; revised estimates are shown in the app.
5.2 After three failed delivery attempts, the order is returned to origin and, for prepaid orders, refunded per the Return & Refund Policy.
5.3 UrbanCart is not liable for delays caused by incorrect or incomplete delivery addresses provided by the customer.
