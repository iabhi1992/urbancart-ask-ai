URBANCART - WARRANTY & REPLACEMENT POLICY
Version: Q2 2024 | Approved by: Customer Experience & Seller Operations
Applies to: Eligible products sold via the UrbanCart mobile app and website in India

1. WARRANTY COVERAGE BY CATEGORY
1.1 Electronics & Gadgets: Manufacturer warranty applies as stated on the product detail page, commonly 6, 12, or 24 months from the date of delivery.
1.2 Home & Kitchen appliances: Warranty of 6 to 24 months where indicated by the manufacturer.
1.3 Apparel, Footwear, and Ethnic Wear: No manufacturer warranty; covered only by the Return & Refund Policy return window.
1.4 A warranty value of 0 months on a product listing means no warranty is offered for that item.

2. WHAT WARRANTY COVERS
2.1 Manufacturing defects and functional failures under normal use.
2.2 Dead-on-arrival units reported within 48 hours of delivery, eligible for immediate replacement.

3. WHAT WARRANTY DOES NOT COVER
3.1 Physical damage, liquid damage, or damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorised repair.
3.2 Normal wear and tear, including battery capacity degradation over time.
3.3 Consumable parts and cosmetic damage that does not affect function.
3.4 Products with a removed, altered, or unreadable serial number.

4. WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS
4.1 Claims are initiated from the Orders screen in the app by selecting the item and choosing Warranty Claim.
4.2 For manufacturer-serviced items, the claim may be routed to the brand's authorised service centre.
4.3 A defect verification is performed before a replacement or repair is approved.

5. REPLACEMENT RULES
5.1 Replacements are issued for the same product and variant, subject to stock.
5.2 If the same variant is unavailable, the customer may choose an alternative variant of equal value or a refund per the Return & Refund Policy.
5.3 Replacement dispatch follows the standard timelines in the Shipping & Delivery Policy.
